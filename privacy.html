Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and security practices. We use Supabase for data storage, which employs enterprise-grade security measures. While we implement reasonable safeguards, no system is 100% secure.

You have the right to:

To exercise these rights, contact us at admin@fiorsaoirse.com.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Generated content is stored for your access until you delete your account. Upon account deletion, we remove your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Our Service is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
